About Us

MedAssist-Sri Lanka at a glance

MedAssist Lanka (Pvt) Ltd (MedAssist- Sri Lanka) is the most unique assistance company of Sri Lanka, duly registered and incorporated under the Companies Act, No. 07 of 2007 of Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as a limited liability company. We specialized in providing Medical, Travel & Legal Assistance for Foreign Tourist during their visit to Sri Lanka. Our assistance service available round the clock for both cooperate members and the individual clients. Our Medical Travel Concierge service enables medical tourism in Sri Lanka by providing services that take out the unknown and provide a hassle free medical travel experience. We facilitate world class healthcare treatments by MedAssist- Sri Lanka selected and certified healthcare providers. Lanka eClinic, the Online-Medical-Clinic managed by MedAssist provides Telemedicine & Mail Order Pharmacy service to manage your medical problems online.
